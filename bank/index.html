CyBorg Bank on Bitcoin Cash smartBCH


Stake CYBORG in Vandals Bank

You can stake any amount of CYBORG token at any time at CyBorg Bank and claim VANDALS tokens as a reward (2.5% of the amount staked) after 7 days.

For the first time you can claim reward immediately after placing your stake (when transaction is confirmed). You need to wait 7 days to claim again.
You can claim reward later (e.g. in a month), but you will receive 2.5% of your balance staked. You can unstake at any time.

You must have MetaMask wallet configured for smartBCH (Bitcoin Cash side-chain) - see below.
Refresh the page if Vandals Bank is not connected to MetaMask. You may need to double-click buttons below.
